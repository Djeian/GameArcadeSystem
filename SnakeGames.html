HOW TO PLAY

• Control the snake using WASD or Arrow Keys.
• Eat the food to grow longer.
• Avoid hitting walls, obstacles, or yourself.
• Each level has 60 seconds.

GAME RULES

• Snake grows when eating food.
• Hitting walls or your body = Game Over.
• Random walls appear every level.
• Walls increase as levels go higher.

GOAL

• Reach the required snake length.
• Each level unlocks NEXT when completed.
• Finish all levels to WIN.
