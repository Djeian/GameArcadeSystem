ABOUT US

🎮 WHAT IS THIS?
Skill Issue Arcade is a mini-game platform designed for students who want to relax, enjoy, and compete in a simple and fun way. It combines different types of games that test memory, speed, and reaction without requiring downloads or high-end devices.

🔥 WHY WE MADE THIS
This project was created to explore if a simple arcade website can actually be fun, useful, and engaging for students. It focuses on enjoyment and accessibility instead of pressure, making it a stress-free environment for everyone.

💡 OUR GOAL
Our goal is to create a smooth and enjoyable arcade experience where players can improve their skills, compete with others, and have fun anytime.
🕹️ WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Play mini-games like Memory, Wordle, and Snake
• Compete and climb the leaderboard (You Must Pay)
• Improve reaction time and memory
• Challenge friends and beat their scores
• Enjoy a clean and simple arcade system

⚡ WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
The platform is designed to be lightweight, fast, and easy to use. It works on most devices and focuses on smooth gameplay and fair competition. Everything is built to be simple but fun.
